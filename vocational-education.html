Introduction

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 has laid emphasis on vocational education and skill development, and highlighted the need for providing vocational exposure to at least 50% students through school and higher education by 2025.

In order to provide a national platform for access to teaching-learning resources (print and digital) on vocational education, a separate vertical has been created on DIKSHA. The teaching-learning resources created by PSS Central Institute of Vocational Education (PSSCIVE), a constituent unit of NCERT located at Bhopal, have been uploaded on this site.

States/UTs are invited to contribute to this platform by submitting the teaching learning resources on vocational education, which will be vetted by PSSCIVE, Bhopal.

For more information about PSSCIVE please visit the website https://www.psscive.ac.in
