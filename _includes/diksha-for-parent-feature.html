Features of DIKSHA for Parent

1

DIKSHA is accessible in English and varied languages. Parent can choose any language depending upon their comfort and convenience. You can access various learning materials through DIKSHA.

2

Find additional materials like videos, interactive content, textbook, assessments etc. around topics that are difficult to understand for their kids & you can store and share content offline, even without Internet connectivity

3

Parents can also scan QR codes in their kids textbook for easy access to the associated lessons on the DIKSHA platform & take revision of lessons that kids learnt in class

4

Parents can follow classroom activities and can clear their doubts outside school hours through one to one sessions with the teachers. DIKSHA is a comprehensive platform for hassle-free interaction of all the stakeholders involved.
