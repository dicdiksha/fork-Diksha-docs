Steps to access NISHTHA Course

Step 01

Registration / Sign In

Do registration or sign in on portal.

Step 02

Search Course

Search for a desired course that you want to take.

Step 03

Register Course

Register for the course that you want to take.

Step 04

Consume Course

Consume all the course contents.

Step 05

Take Assessment

Take the assessment of consumed course.

Step 06

Get Certificate

After successful assessment get your certificate.
